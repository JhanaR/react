ELEMENT: Elements are formed by tags and encompass both the opening and closing tags along with the content. ATTRIBUTE: It define the character of an HTML element. It always placed in the opening tag of an element. It generally provides additional styling (attribute) to the element. Attributes provide additional information or properties to elements, enhancing their functionality or appearance. TAGS: Tags are used to mark up content on UI & define the structure & meaning of the content they enclose. They begin with < symbol and end with > symbol. DIV Group of content together.
